# Research Collaboration Framework — Data Access and IP Boundaries

This document defines the terms under which Synergy Blockchain Pacific (SBP) provides
data access and technical collaboration to research institutions, including those engaged
through the ISOC Foundation programme. It is intended to be read alongside
[BACKGROUND_IP_REGISTRY.md](../BACKGROUND_IP_REGISTRY.md) and
[COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md](../COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md).

---

## What Research Collaborators Receive

Research collaborators engaged under a formal collaboration agreement with SBP receive:

- **Read access to blockchain event data** via permissioned API endpoints, subject to rate
  limits and scope defined in the collaboration agreement
- **Anonymised transaction metrics and workflow completion data** — aggregated and stripped
  of any personally identifiable information prior to sharing
- **Architecture documentation for research methodology purposes** — sufficient detail to
  allow accurate description of the system in academic publications and grant deliverables
- **Portal access for independent verification** — read-only access to relevant portal views
  to allow collaborators to verify claims about system behaviour and performance

---

## What Research Collaborators Do Not Receive

The following are explicitly excluded from any research collaboration arrangement and will
not be shared without a separate written agreement:

- Source code for deployment scripts or admin architecture
- CBS governance decision details or internal policy documents
- Database schemas or direct database access
- Private key material or validator configuration

---

## Joint IP Policy

The following principles govern intellectual property arising from or related to research
collaboration with SBP:

- **Research outputs** (papers, datasets, toolkits, indices) produced by collaborating
  institutions are owned by those institutions for academic purposes and may be published,
  cited, and distributed freely under standard academic norms.
- **Commercialisation of outputs** — any commercialisation of research outputs for Pacific
  government infrastructure deployment, DPI implementation, or CBDC issuance requires a
  separate written agreement with SBP prior to commercialisation.
- **SBP Background IP** as listed in [BACKGROUND_IP_REGISTRY.md](../BACKGROUND_IP_REGISTRY.md)
  is not subject to any joint IP claim arising from research collaboration. Research access
  to SBP systems does not create any equity, licence, or co-ownership interest in that
  Background IP.
